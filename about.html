About

Cubing India aims to support various cubing competitions in India. We aim to bring together number of local cubing communities of different regions. Indian Nationals is the largest most soughed speed cubing competition of the country each year, organized and supported by a team of dedicated volunteers.

Hundreds of Indian & International Speed cubers gather together in the name of a competition for a fun & knowledgeable weekend of solving twisted puzzles. We organize and help local cubing committees across the country giving young minds a chance to increase their spatial IQ and win a title. Join us to be a part of the cubing community in India.

Cubing India is a non-profit organization that mainly works through the grassroot efforts of our volunteers. This organization is dedicated to support and develop speedcubing community in India. We help teams of volunteers and regional cubing communities organize events at different parts of the country to make it accessible to as many cubers as possible. Let’s take time to thank and appreciate the work of our members, without whose help cubing culture couldn’t have been a wide spread success in India.
